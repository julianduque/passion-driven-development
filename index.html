¿Que es Pasión?

Según la Real Academia de la Lengua Española:


¿y que dice la Wikipedia?


La pasión (del verbo en latín, patior, que significa sufrir o sentir) es una emoción definida como un sentimiento muy fuerte hacia una persona, tema, idea u objeto. Así, pues, la pasión es una emoción intensa que engloba el entusiasmo o deseo por algo. El término también se aplica a menudo a un vivo interés o admiración por una propuesta, causa, actividad, etc.

"Passion is the energy that comes from bringing more of YOU into what you do".

Curt Rosengren

¿Que es Passion Driven Development?

Manifesto for Agile Software Development
We are uncovering better ways of developing software by doing it and helping others do it. Through this work we have come to value:

Individuals and interactions over processes and tools
Working software over comprehensive documentation
Customer collaboration over contract negotiation
Responding to change over following a plan

That is, while there is value in the items on the right, we value the items on the left more.
agilemanifesto.org
Manifesto for Passion Driven Development
We are uncovering ways of developing software with passion and fun. Through this work we have come to value:

Empowerment of people over hierarchies
Distributed decision making over centralization
Information transparency over information oligopoly
Capacity development over detailed processes
Participation over estrangement

That is, while there is value in the items on the left, there is no value in the items on the right.
passiondrivendevelopment.org
